The problem
Every morning, most of us reach for our phones before we reach for our coffee. Within minutes we are deep inside an algorithm designed not to inform us, but to capture our attention for as long as possible.

The shift
Early Edge inverts the model. Instead of an algorithm deciding what you see based on what will keep you engaged, you decide what you want to learn — and we find the best of it every single morning.

Goal-aligned, not engagement-driven
You tell us where you are trying to go — your career, your ambitions, the world you want to understand better. Every piece of content we send is chosen because it moves you closer to that goal, not because it is trending.
